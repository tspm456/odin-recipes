Coffee Cake

Description

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour 1 cup and 2 tbsp
  • Room temperature eggs 2
  • Caster sugar 1/2 cup
  • Brown sugar 2tbsp
  • Vanilla 1/2 tbsp
  • Milk 1/2 cup
  • Butter 1/4 cup and 2 tbsp
  • Baking powder 1/2 tsp
  • Salt 1/4 tsp
  • Cinnamon powder 1 tsp

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F
  2. Butter a 6 inch round pan
  3. Mix together cinnamon powder, 2 tbsp melted butter, 1 tbsp brown sugar and 2 tbsp flour in a bowl
  4. Mix together flour, baking soda & salt in a bowl
  5. In a microwave heat milk and butter together till the butter melts
  6. Beat eggs, sugar and vanilla till the mixture is in pale yellow color
  7. Add the milk and butter to the above mix
  8. Fold in flour at the end, do not over mix
  9. Top with cinnamon mix
  10. Bake for 35-40 mins or till a toothpick comes out clean
